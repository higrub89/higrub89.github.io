My story in detail

With a decade-long track record as an elite mechanic, I've forged my career in the art of technical precision, perfecting automotive systems from legendary brands such as MV Agusta, Ducati, Bimota, Ferrari, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Yamaha, Honda, and Aprilia. My experience with these engineering masterpieces has equipped me with a deep understanding of high-end mechanics, an obsessive meticulousness for detail, and an innate ability to solve complex challenges with elegance and efficiency under pressure. Every engine tuned, every diagnostic resolved, has been a testament to my commitment to excellence.

Inspired by innovation, constant evolution, art, the combination of man and machine, and guided by my devotion to God, I have embraced a momentous turning point: the transition to software development, a field where coding precision converges with limitless innovation. At 42 Madrid, I have honed my skills in languages such as C, C++, Python, and Unix systems management, mastering the art of low-level programming and algorithm optimization. My approach combines technical rigor with creativity, transforming challenges into solutions that are as functional as they are elegant.

My vision is clear: to merge my heritage in luxury mechanics with the cutting edge of software development, specializing in embedded systems for the automotive industry. I aspire to design transformative solutions—from intelligent diagnostic systems to architectures for connected and autonomous vehicles—that elevate the driving experience to new heights of perfection. My work is imbued with the same principles that guide my life: sophistication, impeccable discipline, and an unwavering commitment to quality, inspired by biblical ethics and the pursuit of divine excellence.

Like a Swiss watch or a Bach symphony, every line of code I write reflects my passion for precision and my reverence for creation. My impeccable appearance, my love of Baroque classical music, and my unwavering faith in God are the foundation of my identity, shaping a professional who not only builds software, but also creates engineering experiences worthy of Ferrari or Maserati masterpieces. My mission is clear: to bring to the software world the same elegance, functionality, and perfection that define automotive ultra-luxury.
