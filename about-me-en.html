My story in detail

With a solid 10-year track record as a professional mechanic, I have dedicated my career to technical excellence and the perfection of high-end automotive systems for internationally renowned brands. My direct experience with the mechanical complexity of motorcycles, off-road vehicles, and automobiles, including MV Agusta, Ducati, Bimota, Ferrari, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Yamaha, Honda, and Aprilia, has equipped me with an intimate understanding of precision engineering, meticulous attention to detail, and an exceptional ability to diagnose and resolve complex problems under pressure.

My passion for innovation and the constant evolution of the automotive sector was what led me to a turning point. I recognized that the future of the automobile lies as much in hardware as, increasingly, in software. This vision prompted me to undertake a bold and exciting transition into software development, immersing myself in the rigorous and collaborative methodology of **42 Madrid**. Here, I am cultivating and honing my skills in low-level programming with **Linux, C, C++, Python**, **Unix** systems management, and **algorithm** optimization, applying them to programming challenges that demand creativity and efficiency.

My ultimate goal is to merge my deep experience in the world of luxury mechanics with the transformative power of code. I aspire to be a key developer in the automotive industry, creating innovative solutions that address the most complex challenges, from smart diagnostic systems and advanced telematics to software architecture for connected and autonomous vehicles.

As a professional, I am guided by principles of sophistication, flawless functionality, and an unwavering dedication to quality. These values, forged over years of working with automotive excellence, now guide my development as a programmer. My unique background allows me to bring a holistic perspective, understanding not only how software is built but also how it interacts with the physical engineering of the vehicle, ensuring that every line of code contributes to a seamless driving experience. My commitment goes beyond logic; I seek to infuse every project with the same passion for excellence found in a high-precision engine or the design of a classic watch.
